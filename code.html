EdTech Case Study

Thinco Learning Platform

A modern e-commerce and consultation platform designed to simplify haircare routines, connect users with experts, and deliver personalized product experiences.

Thinco came to us with a vision to democratize specialized technical education. Over 12 months, we designed and developed a platform that now serves over 50,000 active students, providing a seamless transition from legacy manual systems to an automated, AI-driven learning environment.

Timeline

Jan 2023 - Dec 2023

Role

Lead UI/UX Designer & Full-stack Engineer

The Challenge

The existing platform was built on outdated PHP infrastructure that struggled with scalability during peak enrollment periods. The user experience was fragmented, requiring students to navigate multiple disconnected portals for course materials, assessments, and community discussions.

  • close High bounce rate (45%) during the mobile checkout process.
  • close Inconsistent design system across web and tablet views.
  • close Generic e-commerce platforms offer no personalization.
  • close Slow page load times (> 4s) impacting SEO rankings.

The Solution

We rebuilt the platform from the ground up using a modern technical stack to ensure future-proofing and high performance. The core focus was on a **Full-stack React Architecture** and a highly modular UI system.
